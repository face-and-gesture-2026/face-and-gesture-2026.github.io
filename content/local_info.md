# To Kyoto City
### From Kansai International Airport (KIX)
Take JR Express “Haruka” bound for Kyoto. You can buy [your ticket online](https://www.westjr.co.jp/global/en/ticket/westqr/) or at the station. You can also [purchase an ICOCA card at the station](https://www.kansai-airport.or.jp/en/faq/1016).

https://maps.app.goo.gl/9KCFvFbVS4LM38qq8
Exit through the single ticket gate and turn left.  Walk straight along the road with the Kyoto Central Wholesale Market on your left (about a 5 minute walk).

### From Kyoto Station (JR-E01) to JR Tambaguchi Station (JR-E03)
At JR Kyoto Station, go to Platforms 31-33 (the San-in / Sagano Line). Take any local train heading toward Sonobe, Kameoka, or Saga-Arashiyama.  Get off at the second stop “Tambaguchi Station” (about a 4 minute ride).
Kyoto Station has JR, Subway, and Kintetsu lines and is numbered as JR-E01, Subway K11, and Kintetsu B01 respectively.

There is “Tamba-**bashi**” Station (not “Tamba-**guchi**”) on the Kintetsu Line from Kyoto Station.  Please be sure to take a JR train bound for Sonobe, Kameoka, or Saga-Arashiyama.
