# Presenter Instructions

## Oral Presentations
Each oral presentation slot is **20 minutes** total, covering the talk, Q&A, and transition to the next
speaker. The breakdown is as follows:

| Phase | Time |
|:-|:-|
| Presentation | ≤ 15 minutes (plan for 14 minutes) |
| Q&A | ~ 4 minutes |
| Transition | ~ 1 minute |

### Guidelines
- Presenters must arrive in the session room **at least 15 minutes before the session begins** to
connect their laptops and test their slides.
- Slides should be in **16:9 widescreen format** (1920×1080 recommended). Presenters use their
own laptops.
- A session chair will introduce each paper and moderate Q&A. The chair will give a **2-minute
warning** and will stop the presentation at 15 minutes to protect Q&A time.
- Videos embedded in slides must be self-contained and must not require an internet connection.
- At least one author must be present in person. Remote or virtual presentations are not
supported.
- Proxy presenters should reach the session chair in advance to notify the session chair of the
proxy presenter’s name.
- The room has an HDMI connection; the audio is also played via the HDMI. We will prepare an
adapter for USB-C, but please bring yours as well.
- Optional: [PPTX](https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/o0bqjsuu7ihlv1qzhf6w0/FG2026_Presentation_template.pptx?rlkey=q5utdv6y5sei7sttsqwx5rylm&dl=0) presentation template


## Poster Presentations
Poster boards are **60″ × 36″ (152 cm × 91 cm)** in **landscape (horizontal) orientation**. Posters must fit
within these dimensions.

### Guidelines
- Prepare your poster in **landscape orientation**. We recommend **A0 size in landscape** (1189 × 841
mm / 46.8″ × 33.1″), which fits the board with comfortable margins.
- Posters will be mounted using the on-site pins provided.
- Authors are expected to be present at their poster for the **full duration of their session**.
- A board number will be assigned to each paper prior to the sessions. Authors should locate their
assigned board and mount their poster **at least 20 minutes before the session begins**.
- Posters must be removed at the end of the session. The organizers are not responsible for
posters left behind.
- Proxy presenters should present each poster.
- Optional: [PPTX](https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/qoaqo117d7y2kwuzrd3q5/FG2026_A0Presentation_template.pptx?rlkey=at4qrmsnry5aajoxlb6zdx2ux&dl=0) poster presentation template
