    To Kyoto City

    From Kansai International Airport (KIX)

    Take JR Express “Haruka” bound for Kyoto. You can buy your ticket online or at the station. You can also purchase an ICOCA card at the station .

    Links
    • Haruka 1-way ticket (QR) (requires mobile internet in Japan)
    • Haruka 1-way e-ticket (needs to be exchanged before use)
    • ICOCA card at KIX
    • ICOCA on Apple Pay

    In Kyoto City

    In KRP campus

    The registration desk is located on the basement floor of Building 4. https://plus.codes/8Q6QXPWQ+3FH

    From JR Tambaguchi Station (JR-E03) to KRP

    https://maps.app.goo.gl/9KCFvFbVS4LM38qq8 Exit through the single ticket gate and turn left. Walk straight along the road with the Kyoto Central Wholesale Market on your left (about a 5 minute walk).

    From Kyoto Station (JR-E01) to JR Tambaguchi Station (JR-E03)

    At JR Kyoto Station, go to Platforms 31-33 (the San-in / Sagano Line). Take any local train heading toward Sonobe, Kameoka, or Saga-Arashiyama. Get off at the second stop “Tambaguchi Station” (about a 4 minute ride). Kyoto Station has JR, Subway, and Kintetsu lines and is numbered as JR-E01, Subway K11, and Kintetsu B01 respectively.

    There is “Tamba-bashi” Station (not “Tamba-guchi”) on the Kintetsu Line from Kyoto Station. Please be sure to take a JR train bound for Sonobe, Kameoka, or Saga-Arashiyama.

    From Subway Shijo (K09) Station to KRP

    https://maps.app.goo.gl/JbPMiw7C1VmqCbE36 At the Shijo-Karasuma bus stop D , take Kyoto City Bus No. 32 bound for “Kyoto Gaidai-mae", and get off at “Kyoto Research Park-mae” (about a 5 minute ride).

    Shijo-Karasuma has bus stops labeled A through F, plus 1 and 2, distributed around the intersection. Please go to Stop D at the south-west corner.

    From Subway Gojo (K10) Station to KRP

    https://maps.app.goo.gl/jLxDH7PVNTPfPGh4A At the Karasuma-Gojo bus stop (No.5) , take Kyoto City Bus No.23 for “Rakusai Bus Terminal" , 43 for ”Kuzebashi Higashizume” , or 80 for “Nishikyogoku” , and get off at “Kyoto Research Park-mae” (about a 5 minute ride).

    Hotel

    For convenient access to KRP, we recommend choosing a hotel near Kyoto Station (JR-E01 == Subway K11), Shijo-Karasuma (Subway K09), or Karasuma-Gojo (Subway K10).
